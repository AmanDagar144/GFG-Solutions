<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/stock-buy-and-sell2615/1">Stock Buy and Sell – Multiple Transaction Allowed</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Medium</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="user-select: auto;"><p style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">The cost of stock on each day is given in an array <strong style="user-select: auto;">price[]</strong>. Each day you may decide to either buy or sell the stock i at <strong style="user-select: auto;">price[i]</strong>, you can even buy and sell the stock on the same day. Find the <strong style="user-select: auto;">maximum profit</strong> that you can get.</span></p>
<p style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">Note: A stock can only be sold if it has been bought previously and multiple stocks cannot be held on any given day.</span></p>
<p style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Input: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">prices[] = [100, 180, 260, 310, 40, 535, 695]</span><br style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Output: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">865</span><br style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Explanation: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18.6667px; user-select: auto;">Buy the stock on day 0 and sell it on day 3 =&gt; 310 – 100 = 210. Buy the stock on day 4 and sell it on day 6 =&gt; 695 – 40 = 655. Maximum Profit = 210 + 655 = 865.<br style="user-select: auto;"><img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/878914/Web/Other/blobid2_1731054745.png" width="324" height="200" style="user-select: auto;"><br style="user-select: auto;"><br style="user-select: auto;"></span><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Input: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">prices[] = [4, 2, 2, 2, 4]</span><br style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Output: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;">2</span><br style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Explanation: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 18.6667px; user-select: auto;">Buy the stock on day 3 and sell it on day 4 =&gt; 4 – 2 = 2. Maximum Profit = 2.</span></pre>
